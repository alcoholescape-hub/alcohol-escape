Your Feelings Are Valid.

Living with or caring about someone with a drinking problem can be confusing, painful, and lonely. It is not your fault. You deserve support and peace, regardless of what they choose to do.

Here are some places to start:

Al-Anon & Alateen

Support groups specifically for the families, friends, and children of people with alcoholism.

Setting Boundaries

Learn how to protect your own mental health and stop the cycle of chaotic behavior.
